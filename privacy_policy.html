We built ShopShare to make list sharing feel effortless and secure. This policy explains exactly what we collect — and what we don’t. anonymous sign‑in availableGoogle sign‑in

📦 What we collect

  • Account information (minimal by design):
    • Anonymous sign‑in: only a private, random identifier — no email, no name.
    • Google sign‑in: we receive your email (used only as a unique account ID), your name, and avatar (to show who’s who in shared lists). That’s it.
  • Your lists & items: the actual content you create (grocery items, notes, shared collaborators).
  • Anonymous analytics & device info: we look at overall usage patterns (which screens are used, app version) to improve the app — this is never tied to your name or email.

🎯 Why we use it

  • To make lists work: sync items instantly across all shared devices — updates appear everywhere.
  • To identify you (only where needed): your email is kept for account uniquess; your name + avatar are shown inside the app so friends know who added “organic avocados”.
  • To improve ShopShare: analytics help us fix bugs and decide what to build next. No profiling, no ads.
  • Rare transactional emails: we may use your email to tell you about security or policy changes — zero marketing spam.

🔐 Sharing your information

We never sell or rent your data. Lists are shared only with people you explicitly invite. We use trusted third‑party services (like hosting and analytics) that are bound by strict data promises — they see only what’s necessary and can’t use it for anything else. If the law requires, we may need to disclose information, but we’ll always push back against overbroad requests.

✅ Your control & security

Easy account deletion — really easy. You can delete your account at any time directly inside the app (look in settings). Once confirmed, your profile, email, and all lists you created are permanently wiped. Lists you shared may stay visible to others only if they were original creators or have copies — that’s the collaborative nature.

Data security: we use modern encryption and follow best practices. While no cloud service is 100% immune, we work hard to keep your information safe.

🕊️ Your rights

You can access and update your name or avatar at any time from the app profile section. Because we keep so little data, there’s not much else — but if you have questions, just ask (contact below).

🧒 Children

ShopShare is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect info from children — if we learn we have, we delete it promptly.

📅 Changes

If we ever update this policy, we’ll post the new version here and update the “last updated” date. If changes are significant, we may also notify users via email or in the app.
